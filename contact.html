Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private and secure?

Yes, absolutely. All your data is stored securely using Google's enterprise-grade infrastructure. We follow strict privacy practices and never share your personal information with third parties. You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time.

How does the AI suggestion feature work?

Our AI suggestions are powered by Google's Vertex AI platform, which analyzes your mood patterns, preferences, and current state to provide personalized wellness recommendations. The AI uses evidence-based mental health practices to suggest activities, resources, and coping strategies.

Is this a replacement for therapy or counseling?

No, our platform is designed to complement, not replace, professional mental health care. While we provide valuable wellness tools and insights, we always recommend consulting with qualified mental health professionals for serious concerns or clinical treatment.

Can parents or guardians access my data?

For users under 18, we follow applicable laws regarding parental consent and access. In general, we prioritize user privacy while ensuring appropriate oversight for minors. Specific policies vary by location and age, and we're happy to discuss your situation individually.

How much does it cost to use this platform?

Our core features are completely free for all users. We believe mental wellness support should be accessible to everyone. Some advanced analytics features may have premium options in the future, but our commitment is to keep essential wellness tools free.

What if I'm having a mental health crisis?

If you're experiencing a mental health emergency or having thoughts of self-harm, please contact emergency services immediately. You can call 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline, text HOME to 741741 for the Crisis Text Line, or call 911 for immediate emergency assistance.

Community Guidelines

When contacting us or using our platform, please remember:

  • Be respectful and kind in all communications
  • Protect your privacy - avoid sharing sensitive personal information
  • Use appropriate language suitable for a youth-focused platform
  • Report any concerning behavior or safety issues
  • Remember that our platform is for wellness support, not crisis intervention
